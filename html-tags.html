3.Paragraph Tag

Used to add paragraphs in HTML

<p> This is a sample paragraph </p>

4.Anchor Tag

Used to add links to your page

<a href="https://google.com"> Google </a>

How it looks

5.Image Tag

Used to add links to your page

<img src="/image.png" alt="Description">

6.Br Tag

Used to add next line(line breaks) to your page

<br>

7.Bold, Italic & Underline Tags

Used to highlight text in your page

<b> Bold </b>
<i> Italic </i>
<u> Underline </u>

8.Big & Small Tags

Used to display big & small text on your page

<big> Big </big>
<small> Small </small>

9.Hr Tag

Used to display a horizontal ruler, used to separate content

<hr>

10.Subscript & Superscript Tag

Used to display a horizontal ruler, used to separate content

<sub> subscript </sub>
<sup> superscript </sup>
